Branding

The brandmark is the primary element of the Debut identity. However, other components parts play an important role in establishing the Debut visual style. These elements indluce: colour, typograpohy, imagery and tone of voice

The Brandmark

The brandmark is the most visible element of our identity - a universal signature across all communications. It can appear in mono, colour, or reversed out of imagery but the primary logo colour is black.
