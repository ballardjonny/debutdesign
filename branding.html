Branding

The brandmark is the most visible element of our identity - a universal signature across all communications. It can appear in mono, colour, or reversed out of imagery but the primary logo colour is black.

The brandmark is the primary element of that identity. However, other components parts play an important role in establishing the Debut visual style. These elements indluce: colour, typograpohy, imagery and tone of voice
