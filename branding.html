Marketing & Brand

The brandmark is the primary element of the Debut identity. However, other components parts play an important role in establishing the Debut visual style. These elements indluce: colour, typograpohy, imagery and tone of voice

The Brandmark

The brandmark is the most visible element of our identity - a universal signature across all communications. It can appear in mono, colour, or reversed out of imagery but the primary logo colour is black.

Rules of Use

The logo should always have breathing space. This can be determined by using the height of the letter ‚u‚ all the way around (see next page). The minimum recommended size for the logo is 30mm wide (150 pixels online). It should never be used below 10mm wide (75 pixels online).

Logo Guidelines

It is important that the appearance of the brandmark remains consistent. The brandmark should not be misinterpreted, modified or added to.
