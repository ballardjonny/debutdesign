About

These brand guidelines have been created to serve the purpose of creating a uniform brand experience across all our customer and client touchpoints. We are a youthful brand, creating an aesthetic that echos youth culture with our millenial audience. While doing so, we also aim to convey the underlying seriousness and credibility of our service in shaping graduate futures.

A working document

This document is created with the view to evolve, adapt, and solidify over time. While the core principles with likely remain, elements are open to adjustment while the brand establishes.

Debut Design - by Jonny Ballard.
