Typography

Poppins & Open Sans are the standard typeface for Debut on the web, and system fonts are used on the iOS and Android apps.

Poppins

The Poppins type is reserved for headings and standout text only. The only font weight that should be used with this font is 'Semi-Bold'


Download Google Font

Open Sans

Open Sans is used for all other scenarios including body copy and subtitles. Details on sizing and font weight are outlined below.
